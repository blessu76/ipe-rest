Editor's note

idle process
The name is a computing term: the state a processor enters when it finally has nothing left to do — and simply rests.
We build for the moment the work is done. Music that lets a tired mind exhale. Small tools that think on your own device, so your quiet thoughts stay quiet. No dashboards begging for your attention — just a little rest, written into the code.

There's a stillness underneath it all — be still, and know — carried gently, never preached. It's simply the reason the whole studio breathes the way it does.
